Kanye West: A Brief History

Ye, or more commonly known as Kanye West, is an American rapper and producer. Although he initially began making waves in the music industry through his unique producing style, he always had aspirations of becoming a rapper. Eventually, Ye was given the opportunity to rap. His first album, The College Dropout, gained praise everywhere and has become one of the most critically acclaimed albums in all of hip-hop.
